Biography

I am a PhD candidate in political science at the University of Kansas. I conduct research on local government and criminal justice reform by investigating the motivations for and mechanisms to implementing alternative sentencing schemes, such as criminal diversion programs. I also teach courses in American politics and public policy at the University of Kansas and Kansas State University.
